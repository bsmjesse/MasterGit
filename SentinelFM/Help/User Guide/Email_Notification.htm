Email Notification

When a person's email address has been added to the bottom of the maintenance Service Info screen that person will automatically be notified when a vehicle is approaching a service interval and warned when it has reached a service interval.

 

An email from SentinelFM@hgiwireless.com with a subject of Maintenance Notification will be received when the vehicle odometer has reached 15% of the maintenance interval specified.  An example of a maintenance notification email is illustrated below:

 

Subject:  Maintenance Notification

Date/Time: 6/22/2006 8:30:13 AM

Vehicle [Paul Box] is approaching scheduled maintenance. Current Odometer reading: [244100].

 

 

An email from SentinelFM@hgiwireless.com with a subject of Maintenance Warning will be received when the vehicle odometer has reached 100% of the maintenance interval specified.  An example of a maintenance warning email is illustrated below:

 

Subject:  Maintenance Warning

Date/Time: 6/22/2006 8:34:25 AM

Vehicle [Paul Box] is due for scheduled maintenance. Current Odometer reading: [259000]
