Viewing Stop Sequence History

On the map in the History screen, you can choose a vehicle and then view each location where it stopped on specific dates that you choose.

 

  • For an in-depth explanation of the History screen, including navigating to the Stop Sequence map and grid, and viewing a breadcrumb trail, see History -Introduction.

  • To view each location where messages, commands, GPS positions, triggered sensors, and other activity history were communicated. see Viewing Vehicle Path History.

 

When you choose to display history with the Stop Sequence option, on the map, the colours of the icons reflect the number of minutes that the vehicle was stopped. To view an explanation of the colours, click the Legend button . The Legends screen appears, with a key to the colours. To return to the History screen, click Close.

 

Sentinel FM assigns a number to each stop. Number 1 is the first stop made by the vehicle during the time period you chose. These numbers appear in the No column in the grid. To have the map display these numbers, too, click Map Options, and enable the Show Sequence No. option.

 

 

To view stop sequence history, follow procedures in History -Introduction. Once you click View, the history for the date, time, and vehicle that you chose is displayed in two ways:

 

  • On the map, an icon marks each location where the vehicle stopped.

  • In the corresponding grid, each row gives information about one stop. Stops are listed from the most recent, at the top, down to the oldest. The grid contains the following columns:

 

Name of Column

Function

Details

No

Shows the number assigned by Sentinel FM to this stop.

Number 1 is the first stop made by the vehicle during the specified time period.

Arrival

Shows the date and time the vehicle arrived at the stop.

 

Address

Shows the address (or landmark, if configured to show landmarks) of the stop.

This field can be configured to show street address or nearest landmark. See Changing User Preferences

Departure

Shows the date and time the vehicle left the stop.

 

Duration

Shows the duration of the stop.

 

Status

Options are:

  • Stopped - Ignition was turned off

  • Idling - Ignition was on for more than 3 minutes, but the vehicle was not moving  

 

(no column heading)

To clearly view one position of the vehicle, click Map at the end of the row that shows the vehicle stop. No other positions are displayed on the map.  In the grid, the row is highlighted.

To return to viewing all positions at once, click Show All. The map displays the positions of the vehicle at the time of all stops listed.
