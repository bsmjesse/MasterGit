Changing Map Viewing Options

You can change how you want the Map screen to appear. By default, vehicle names, landmarks, and landmark names are all displayed. You can choose to hide any of these.

 

 

 

 

To begin working in the Map screen, see Displaying Vehicles in the Map Screen. In the Map screen, click on the Map Options button.  In the Map Options screen, enable or disable any of the following:

 

 

  • Show Vehicle names

  • Show Landmarks

  • Show Landmark names

 

Click Save. Your changes are reflected in the Map screen.
