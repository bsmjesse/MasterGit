History - Introduction

In the History screen, you can view the path that a vehicle took on a specific date or range of dates that you choose. The path is made up of individual locations. On the map, you can view the locations, and in the grid below the map, you can view information about each location. You can only view the history of one vehicle at a time.

Choosing History Type Options

You can choose between four ways of tracing vehicle paths ("history types"):

  1. Vehicle Path - On the map, displays each location where a communication to or from the vehicle took place. In the corresponding grid, each row is one communication. For details on the Vehicle Path map and grid, see Viewing Vehicle Path History.

  2. Stop and Idle Sequence - On the map,  displays each location where the vehicle stopped or idled.  In the corresponding grid, each row is one place where the vehicle stopped or idled.  

  3. Stop Sequence - On the map, displays each location where the vehicle stopped. In the corresponding grid, each row is one place where the vehicle stopped. For details on the Stop Sequence map and grid, see Viewing Stop Sequence History.

  4. Idle Sequence, - On the map, displays each location where the vehicle idled.  In the corresponding grid, each row is one place where the vehicle idled.

Map Functions in the History Screen

Many map functions in the History screen are the same as in the Map screen. For details on basic map functions, see Centering, Zooming and Other Map Functions.

 

When you first display history, all locations are visible in the center of the map. Sometimes as you navigate through the map, the map may move so that you are no longer able to view the area of the activities you are monitoring. To re-display all locations, click the show all button . All locations appear in the center of a new map display.

 

The map can only display communications or stops that have an address.

 

On the map, each location where a communication or a stop took place is marked with a circular colored icon that is blank or has an interior arrow indicating direction of travel  ( or ). A User can move the mouse over an icon to view details. If more than one communication or stop took place in the same location, the mouseover displays a list of all the communications or stops that were made in that location.

Linking Vehicle Locations with a Breadcrumb Trail

You can choose to have the locations for either history type linked graphically with a "breadcrumb trail", a dotted line that traces the path taken by the vehicle during that time period. To do this, on the left of the map, click the Map Options button. Enable the Show BreadCrumb Trail option.

Working in the History Screen

To view history:

 

  1. From the home page, click History. The History screen appears.

  2. On the right side of the screen, specify the history parameters you want to display.

    • In the From and To fields, specify a range of time by entering start and end dates and times. For dates, you can click in the calendar.

    • From the History Type list, choose whether to display a series of communications for a vehicle (Vehicle Path) or a series of stops that a vehicle made (Stop Sequence). Each one displays a grid with different columns.

    • From the Fleet list, choose the fleet for the vehicle whose history you want to display.

    • From the Vehicle list, choose the vehicle whose history you want to display.

    • If Stop Sequence was selected choose the stop duration filter to be applied

  1. Click View. The history for the date, time, and vehicle that you chose is displayed in two ways, on the map and in the grid.

    • For information on the Vehicle Path option, see Viewing Vehicle Path History.

    • For information on the Stop Sequence option, see Viewing Stop Sequence History.
