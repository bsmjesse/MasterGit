Viewing Sent Messages

You can view sent messages in several places:

 

  • Messages that were sent within the last 24 hours are displayed in the scrolling list at the centre right of the Map screen.  Messages in this screen include all incoming messages sent from vehicles that belong to the fleet you are authorized to view.

 

          

 

  • Messages from other time periods are displayed in either the (main) Messages screen or the History screen. The Messages screen displays all messages, both incoming and outgoing, with message details and response details when available. The History screen displays messages ("MDTTextMessage" in the Message Type field, without any details) along with commands, GPS positions, and other vehicle activities. For details on the History screen, see History-Introduction.

 

You can view messages from several vehicles at the same time.

 

Messages can be incoming or outgoing. "Incoming" messages are ones that are sent from a vehicle to Sentinel FM. "Outgoing" messages are from Sentinel FM to a vehicle. In the Direction column in the Messages screen, and in the Message Type column in the History screen, incoming messages are represented by <<; outgoing messages are represented by >>.

 

To view sent messages in the Messages screen:

 

  1. From the home page, click Messages. The Messages screen appears.

  2. At the top of the screen, choose the messages you want to display.

    • In the From and To fields, specify a range of time for sent messages by choosing start and end dates and times. For dates, you can click in the calendar.

    • In the Fleet and Vehicle fields, choose whose messages you want to display. You can view messages for "All Vehicles" and for "Entire Fleet", or, to narrow your search, choose a fleet and a vehicle.

    • In the Folder List field, you can narrow your search by choosing to display only incoming or only outgoing messages.

  1. Click View Text Messages. A grid appears displaying the messages that you chose. In the grid, note the following columns:

    • In the Date column, view the date and time messages were sent. To view details about a message, click in this column. The Message Info screen appears where you can view the license plate number of the vehicle, the complete text of the message and its response, etc. To return to the Messages screen, click Close.

    • In the From and To columns, view the names of the vehicles or the logins of the users who sent and received messages. A blank in the To column, normal for all incoming messages, indicates that the message was sent to Sentinel FM, not to a specific user.

    • In the Direction column, view whether the messages were incoming (<<) or outgoing (>>).

    • In the Message column, view the text of messages that were sent. If you need to see the complete text of a message, and it is too long to be displayed here, click in the Date column to see that message displayed in the Message Info screen.

    • In the Response column, view any responses that were sent to messages.

    • In the Ack column, view any acknowledgements by the vehicle box that were sent in response to messages.

    • In the Read column, view the names of users who read messages.

 

For help on sending a new message, see Sending a New Message.
