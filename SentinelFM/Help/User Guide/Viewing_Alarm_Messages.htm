Viewing Alarm Messages

Alarm messages can be viewed, accepted and closed by accessing the Sentinel FM Messages function and selecting the Alarms option.  

 

You can view alarm messages in several places of the Sentinel FM system:

 

  • Alarm messages that were sent within the last 24 hours are displayed in the scrolling list at the centre right of the Map screen.  Messages in this screen include all incoming alarm messages sent from vehicles that belong to the fleet you are authorized to view.

          

  • Alarm messages from other time periods can be displayed using the Messages function. The Messages screen displays all incoming alarm messages, and message details. The History screen displays messages ("Alarm" in the Message column, and  details of the message in the MsgDetails column) along with commands, GPS positions, and other vehicle activities. For details on the History screen, see History-Introduction.

 

You can view alarm messages from several vehicles at the same time.

 

Alarm messages are incoming messages that are sent from a vehicle to Sentinel FM. In the Message Type column in the History screen, incoming messages are represented by <<.

 

To view incoming alarm messages in the Messages screen:

 

  1. From the home page, click Messages. The Messages screen appears.

  2. At the top of the screen, choose the Alarms option.

    • In the From and To fields, specify a range of time for sent messages by choosing start and end dates and times. For dates, you can click in the calendar.

  1. Click View Alarms. A grid appears displaying the alarm messages that you chose. In the grid, note the following columns:

    • In the Date/Time column, view the date and time messages were sent.

    • In the Vehicle column, view the names of the vehicles that sent the messages.

    • In the Description column, the details of the alarm message are listed

    • In the Level column, the severity level of the alarm is listed.

    • In the State column, the state of the alarm (New, Accepted or Closed) is listed.

 

  1. To Accept an alarm or alarms, select the checkbox(s) next to the alarm(s) to be accepted and click on the "Accept Alarms" button

 

  1. To Close an alarm or alarms, select the checkbox(s) next to the alarm(s) to be closed and click on the "Close Alarms" button
