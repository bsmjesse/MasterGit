Generating Reports

Sentinel-FM offers a variety of custom reports that can be output in several standard formats.

 

To generate a report:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the Reports hyperlink on the top of the page. The Reports page appears.

  2. From the Report drop-down list, choose the report type you want to generate. For details on each report, including options that you can configure, see Report Descriptions.

  3. If applicable, in the From and To fields, specify a date/time range by choosing from and to dates and times. For dates, you can click in the calendar. For times choose from the drop-down list.

  4. In the Fleet and Vehicle lists, choose the fleet and vehicle(s) that you want to report on.

  5. In the Format list, choose an output format. For details, see Output Format Descriptions.

  6. Select the Preview button to generate and view the report.  

  7. Depending on the output format selected you may be able to save and/or print out the report.

  8. Use the Back button located in the bottom right of the page to return to the main Sentinel-FM reports page.
