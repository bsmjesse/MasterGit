Assigning GeoZones to Vehicles

 

Note: Before assigning GeoZones, you must set them up. For details, see Setting Up GeoZones.

 

In Sentinel FM, once you have set up GeoZones, you can assign one or more of them to a vehicle. You can assign the same GeoZone to more than one vehicle. You can also remove GeoZones from the vehicle. (For more about fleets and vehicles, see Displaying Vehicles on the Map Screen.)

 

While you are assigning or removing GeoZones for a vehicle, you can also override the default severity level that was assigned to the vehicle. For details, see Changing Severity Levels for GeoZones.

Assigning GeoZones to a Vehicle

To assign GeoZones to a vehicle:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink on the top of the screen. Choose the Vehicle-GeoZone Assignment tab.

  2. Choose a fleet, then a vehicle. Wait for the lists of assigned and unassigned GeoZones to appear:

 

 

  • To add one or more GeoZones to the current vehicle, in the Unassigned GeoZones list (on the left), select the GeoZones you want to assign to the current vehicle. Click Add->.

  • To add all GeoZones in the Unassigned GeoZones list to the current vehicle, click Add All->.

  1. In the Assigned GeoZones list (on the right), the GeoZones that you selected appear in a table.

  2. In the Severity column, view the severity of the GeoZone. To modify the severity level, see Changing Severity Levels for GeoZones.

  3. In the Status column, view the status of this assignment. At this stage, the status is "Add Pending", meaning that the GeoZones that you selected have not yet been assigned to the vehicle.

  1. To confirm your assignments, click the Sync button. A message is sent to the box for that vehicle with information about the new status of its GeoZones. The Status column displays the success or failure of the message.  Once a GeoZone has been successfully assigned to the vehicle the Status is changed to Sync.
