Setting Time Zones, Units of Measurement, and the Default Fleet

To set any of these options, from the home page, click Configuration. On the User Preferences tab, click Edit and:

  • In the Time Zone field, choose the time zone where you are located. Times displayed in the vehicle grid in the Map screen, in Reports, and in History will all be shown in this time zone.

NOTE: Times displayed list the Greenwich Mean Time zone before the more familiar name. For instance, Eastern Standard Time is GMT-5; Pacific Standard Time is GMT-8.

  • Enable or disable the Daylight Savings Time checkbox.

NOTE: This must be done manually whenever the clocks change.

  • In the Units field, choose kilometers or miles. Distances shown in the vehicle grid in the Map screen, in Reports, and in History will all be converted to this unit.

  • In the Default Fleet field, choose the fleet that you want to automatically appear when you open the Map screen.

  • By default, the vehicle grid in the Map screen shows the address of each vehicle. If you prefer that the nearest landmark be displayed instead of the address, enable the Show Landmark Name Instead of An Address checkbox.

Click Save.
