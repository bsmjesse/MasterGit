Setting Up GeoZones

If you want to have an alarm triggered when a vehicle comes into or goes out of certain areas, you can set up GeoZones. Rectangular or polygon (maximum 10 point) GeoZones can be positioned anywhere you want on the map. For each GeoZone, you must specify:

 

  • A name (and description, if desired) for the GeoZone;

  • Whether an alarm should be triggered for this GeoZone, and if so, what vehicle movement (for example, entering or exiting) will trigger it;

  • The level of severity (No Alarm, Notify, Warning, or Critical) of the alarm that occurs when a vehicle enters or exits the GeoZone (This is the default severity level for this GeoZone, but you can override it for individual vehicles. For details, see Changing Severity Levels for GeoZones.);

  • An email address if notification of GeoZone alarm trigger is required

 

To view a detailed map of all GeoZones, see Viewing Landmarks and GeoZones.

 

To assign GeoZones to vehicles, see Assigning GeoZones to Vehicles.

 

Step 1. Enter basic information for a GeoZone.

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click the Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink the top of the screen. On the GeoZone tab, view the table of GeoZones that have been entered. If you do not see the table, click Cancel, and the screen will refresh, displaying the table.

  • To add a new GeoZone, click on the Add GeoZone button below the table.

  • To modify a GeoZone, in the table, click the edit icon () in the row for that GeoZone.

  • To delete a GeoZone, in the table, click the delete icon () in the row for that GeoZone. You are prompted to confirm the deletion.

  1. If you are adding or modifying a GeoZone, an add/modify screen appears. Enter information in the GeoZone Name and GeoZone Description fields.

Step 2. Specify the alarm trigger and severity.

If you do not want an alarm to be triggered for this GeoZone, from the Direction list, choose Disable. Alarms for this GeoZone will be disabled.

 

If you do want an alarm to be triggered:

 

  1. To specify the vehicle movements that you want to trigger an alarm, from the Direction list, choose one of the following:

    • entering the GeoZone - choose In

    • exiting the GeoZone - choose Out

    • either entering or exiting the GeoZone - choose InOut

  2. To specify the severity of the alarm that will be triggered, from the Severity list, choose the level that you want for this GeoZone. For details about severity levels, see Alarm Severity Levels.

  3. Specify an email address for alarm notification as well as time zone of the addressee and the type of alarm that should cause email notification.

Step 3. Draw the GeoZone.

In the middle of the add/modify screen, click on the DRAW GEOZONE hyperlink. The hyperlink displays the page where you can draw the GeoZone.

 

Use the standard map zoom tools to zoom into the area where the GeoZone is to be drawn or select a landmark from the Map To: drop down list to automatically zoom into a specific landmark area.

 

Once the approximate location has been selected choose whether you want to draw a Rectangle or Polygon GeoZone.

By Rectangle

  1. Once you have selected the Rectangle option click on the Start drawing button

  2. Draw a rectangle by moving the mouse pointer to one corner of the rectangle to be drawn then holding down the left mouse button drag the mouse pointer to the other corner and release the mouse button.

  3. A black rectangle with a dotted interior will appear.

  4. If you are not satisfied with the GeoZone you can click on the Clear Map button to clear the rectangle and start the process over again by clicking on the Start drawing button to specify a new rectangle.

  5. When finished click on the Complete Drawing button

  6. Click on the Save button  ("GeoZone was drawn successfully" message will be displayed)

  7. Click on the Save button ("GeoZone has been updated successfully" message will be displayed)

By Polygon

  1. Once you have selected the Polygon option click on the Start drawing button

  2. Click on the first point of the polygon (a black dot will appear)

  3. Click on the next point of the polygon (a red line with connect between the first and second points)

  4. Continue clicking on subsequent points in the polygon to a maximum of 10

  5. A any point during the process of specifying points you are not satisfied with the polygon being drawn you can click on the Clear Map button to clear the screen and start the process over again by clicking on the Start drawing button

  6. When finished click on Complete Drawing button (last point and first point will be joined creating closed shape)

  7. A red polygon with a striped interior will appear.

  8. Click on Save button ("GeoZone was drawn successfully" message will be displayed)

  9. Click on the Save button ("GeoZone has been updated successfully" message will be displayed)
