Removing GeoZones from a Vehicle

 

To remove GeoZones from a vehicle:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink on the top of the screen. Choose the Vehicle-GeoZone Assignment tab.

  2. Choose a fleet, then a vehicle. Wait for the lists of assigned and unassigned GeoZones to appear.

  3. To remove one GeoZone at a time from the current vehicle, in the Assigned GeoZones list (on the right), click "Delete" in the row for the GeoZone you want to remove.

    • If the GeoZone you are removing had a pending status, it is moved to the Unassigned GeoZones list (on the left).

    • If the GeoZone you are removing had another status, it remains in the Assigned GeoZones list, but in the Status column, it is listed as "Delete Pending".  

  4. To remove all GeoZones from the current vehicle, click <-Remove All.

  1. To confirm your assignments, click the Sync button. The assigned GeoZones are moved into the list of unassigned GeoZones. At the same time, a message is sent to the box for that vehicle with information about the new status its of GeoZones. The Status column displays the success or failure of the message.
