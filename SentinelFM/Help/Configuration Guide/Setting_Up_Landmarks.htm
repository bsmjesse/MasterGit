Setting Up Landmarks


For each landmark, you can specify:

 

  • landmark name (and description, contact name, and phone, if desired)

  • radius of the landmark, that is, the distance around the landmark that are considered part of the landmark itself. This is used to determine if a vehicle is at or near a landmark and therefore the landmark will show in the vehicle grid for that vehicle. For example, if you create Landmark X and specify a radius of 50 meters, then, for any vehicle that is within 50 meters of that landmark, the Address column of the vehicle grid will display "Landmark X" rather than a street address. For vehicles outside of the radius of any landmarks, the Address column will display a street address.

  • location of the landmark, identified either by street address or by its geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) or by physically identifying a location on the map.

  • An email address of a person that should be contacted when a vehicle is at the landmark.

Adding, Editing or Deleting a Landmark

To add, edit, or delete a landmark:

Step 1. Enter basic information for the landmark.

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the  Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink on the top of the screen. On the Landmarks tab, view the table of landmarks that have been entered. If you do not see the table, click Cancel, and the screen will refresh, displaying the table.

  • To add a new landmark, click on the Add Landmark button located below the bottom of the table.

  • To modify a landmark, in the table, click the edit icon () in the row for that landmark.

  • To delete a landmark, in the table, click the delete icon () in the row for that landmark. You are prompted to confirm the deletion.

  • To view the landmark on a map select the "Map It" hyperlink.

  1. If you are adding or modifying a landmark, an add/modify screen appears. In the Landmark Name, Landmark Description, Contact Name, and Phone fields, enter name and contact information.

  2. In the Radius field, enter the distance around the landmark that are considered part of the landmark itself. (For details, see Landmark Requirements.)


Step 2. Specify the location.

At the top of the add/modify screen, enable an option (By Street Address or By Coordinates/Map) for entering the location of the landmark. Wait for the new screen to appear.

By Street Address

  1. In the Street, City, State (Prov), and Country fields, enter the address of the landmark. If you aren't sure of the complete address, enter part of it and click Save.

  2. A list of possible addresses appears. To choose one, click the word Select that appears to the right of the address. The screen refreshes and your choice is highlighted (you may have to scroll down to see it).

  3. To confirm your choice, click Save again.

By Coordinates/Map

In the Latitude and Longitude fields, enter the appropriate coordinates in decimal degrees (north and east are positive). Or, for help with specifying latitude and longitude by physically locating on a map:

 

  1. Click VIEW MAP. A popup map screen appears.

  2. In the map, zoom in on the area where you want to create a landmark. For details, see Centering, Zooming, and Other Map Functions.

  3. Using the mouse, click the location on the map where you want to enter a landmark. The screen refreshes and the map reappears with the landmark displayed as a yellow square in the middle of the screen.

  4. To save the landmark, click Save. To cancel it, click Cancel . If Save is selected you are returned to the add/modify screen, where the Latitude and Longitude fields are now populated.

Step 3. Enter an optional contact email address

In the Email field, enter the email address of a person that should be contacted when a vehicle is at the landmark.  Also select the correct time zone of the contact and whether the times should be automatically adjusted for daylight savings time.


Step 4: Save and view your landmark

To save the landmark you have added or modified, click Save. To cancel it, click Cancel. You are returned to the table of landmarks, where you can view the changes you made. To view landmarks on maps, see The Main Map Screen and Viewing Landmarks and GeoZones.

 

Note: Once you save, if you added information by street address, Sentinel FM automatically populates the Latitude and Longitude fields. If you added information by coordinates, Sentinel FM automatically populates the street address.
