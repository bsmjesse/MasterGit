Changing Severity Levels for GeoZones

When you set up a GeoZone, you assign a default severity level to it. For details, see Setting Up GeoZones. However, you can override the default severity level for a GeoZone that has been assigned to an individual vehicle.

 

For details about severity levels, see Alarm Severity Levels.

 

To override the GeoZone's default severity level for an individual vehicle:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink on the top of the screen. Choose the Vehicle-GeoZone Assignment tab.

  2. Choose a fleet, then a vehicle. Wait for the lists of assigned and unassigned GeoZones to appear. To assign a GeoZone to the vehicle, or to remove one, see Assigning GeoZones to Vehicles.

 

 

  1. In the Assigned GeoZones list (on the right), the GeoZones that are assigned appear in a table. In the Severity column, view the severity of the GeoZone. To modify the severity level, in the row for that GeoZone, click "Edit".

  2. The screen refreshes and you can choose a different severity level from a drop down list. To confirm your change, click "Update" in the row for that GeoZone. The severity level is now changed for the vehicle. You are returned to the original table.
