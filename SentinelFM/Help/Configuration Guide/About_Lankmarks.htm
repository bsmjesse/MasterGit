About Landmarks


Use landmarks to quickly refer to or locate frequently-used locations such as your main office, branch offices, a customer's office, etc. You can also use a landmark to help define a GeoZone. (For details, see Setting Up GeoZones.)

 

Landmarks can be positioned anywhere you want on the map data either by street address or by coordinates or by physically specifying a location on a map. On the Map screen, they look like this:
