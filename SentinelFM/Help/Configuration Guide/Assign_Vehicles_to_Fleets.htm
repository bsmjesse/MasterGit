Assigning Vehicles to Fleets

In Sentinel-FM, you can group together the vehicles that you want to track or map at the same time by assigning them to a fleet. You can also remove vehicles from a fleet. Vehicles can be in more than one fleet at a time. For more information about fleets and vehicles, see Displaying Vehicles on the Map Screen.

 

To assign vehicles to a fleet, or remove them from a fleet:

 

  • From any Sentinel-FM page, click the Configuration hyperlink on the top of the screen. On the Vehicles tab, choose the Fleet-Vehicle Assignment sub tab.

  • In the Fleets field, choose the fleet you want to work with. Wait for the lists of assigned and unassigned vehicles to appear.

  • To add a  vehicle to the current fleet, highlight it in the Unassigned Vehicle list and click Add->. To add all vehicles listed to the current fleet, click Add All->.

  • To remove a vehicle from the current fleet, highlight it in the Assigned Vehicle list and click <-Remove. To remove all vehicles listed from the current fleet, click <-Remove All.

  • The vehicles are moved into the list you chose.
