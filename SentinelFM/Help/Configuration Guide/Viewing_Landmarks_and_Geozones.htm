Viewing Landmarks and GeoZones

  • For information about landmarks, see Setting up Landmarks.

  • For information about GeoZones, see Setting up GeoZones.

 

To view a detailed map of all landmarks and GeoZones:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the  Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink on the top of the screen. On the Map tab, choose whether to enable Show Landmarks, Show GeoZones, or both.

  2. Zoom in on the area where you want to view landmarks or GeoZones. For details, see Centering, Zooming, and Other Map Functions.

 

Note: You can also view landmarks in the main Map screen.
