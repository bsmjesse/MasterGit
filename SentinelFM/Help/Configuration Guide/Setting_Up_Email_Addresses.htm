Emails for Alarm Notification

You can enter email addresses for people who you want to be contacted when a sensor is triggered in a fleet. For each email address that you enter, you can specify the level of severity (Critical, Warning, Notify, or No Alarm) of the alarm that requires sending an email to the contact. The email will include the date, time, vehicle, and other information about the sensor that was triggered. For details about severity levels, see Alarm Severity Levels.

 

Email addresses can be for standard email programs, alphanumeric pagers, or cell phones that support text messaging.

 

You can specify the time zone that will display for emails to each address, so that the time the alarm occurred is shown to the person receiving the email in their own time zone. Time zones are shown in Greenwich Mean Time. For instance, Eastern Standard Time is GMT-5; Pacific Standard Time is GMT-8. You can manually enable or disable the daylight savings time feature.

 

To enter email addresses:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the Configuration hyperlink on the top of the screen. Select the Fleets tab and then the Email Addresses sub tab;

  2. From the Fleet drop-down menu choose a fleet. If email addresses are already entered for this fleet, they appear in a table.

  3. To add a new email address, click Add Email Address. In the fields that appear below the table, enter information for this address.

  4. To modify an email address, in the table, click the edit icon () in the row for that address. Modify the information for this address.

  5. To delete an email address, in the table, click the delete icon () in the row for that address. You are prompted to confirm the deletion.

  1. For each address that you add or modify, specify:

  2. the email address

  3. the time zone information

  4. enable or disable auto adjustment for Daylight Savings time

  5. the severity level or levels (Critical, Warning, or Notify) that should trigger an email to this address

  1. To save:

  2. If you are adding a new email address, click Save.

  3. If you are editing an existing email address, in the table, click the check () next to the address to save.
