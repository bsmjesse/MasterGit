Assigning Vehicles to Fleets

In application, you can group together the vehicles that you want to track or map at the same time by assigning them to a fleet. You can also remove them from a fleet. Vehicles can be in more than one fleet at a time. For more about fleets and vehicles, see Displaying Vehicles on the Map Screen.

 

To assign vehicles to a fleet, or remove them from a fleet:

 

  1. From the home page, click Configuration. On the Vehicles tab, choose the Fleet-Vehicle Assignment tab.

  2. In the Fleet field, choose the fleet you want to work with. Wait for the lists of assigned and unassigned vehicles to appear.

  • To add a  vehicle to the current fleet, highlight it in the Unassigned Vehicle list and click Add. To add all vehicles listed to the current fleet, click Add All.

  • To remove a vehicle from the current fleet, highlight it in the Assigned Vehicle list and click Remove. To remove all vehicles listed from the current fleet, click Remove All.

  1. The vehicles are moved into the list you chose.
