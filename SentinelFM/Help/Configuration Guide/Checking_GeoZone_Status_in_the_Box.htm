Checking GeoZone Status in the Box

The Check Sync button permits verification of GeoZone status inside a box.  A message is sent from the Server to the box confirming that the assigned GeoZone(s) was successfully added or deleted.  After confirmation (CheckSync) command is executed - the "Assigned Geo-Zones" Table on the right show the true status of the GeoZones inside the box.   It is recommended to "Check Sync" before assigning GeoZones and after every GeoZone assignment to insure that the box successfully accepted the commands.

 

The check Sync function can be accessed:

 

  1. From any Sentinel-FM page, click on the Landmarks & GeoZones hyperlink on the top of the screen. Choose the Vehicle-GeoZone Assignment tab.

  2. Choose a fleet, then a vehicle. Wait for the lists of assigned and unassigned GeoZones to appear.

  3. Click on the Check Sync button

  4. The box will be contacted and the list of assigned GeoZones will be updated
